shipping and delivery

shipping rates and time

Shipping times depend on the value and rarity of the item. To find out an exact time of shipping, follow these steps.

1. Have an item in your cart, proceed to checkout.

2. Enter your address.

3. Select a shipping option.

4. Select payment method.

Shipping and handling costs are found under the order summary section.

refund policy

Everything follows a 30 day return policy. Any special exceptions will be mentioned in the order.

track your package

1. Go to your orders.

2. Click on the order you'd like to track.

3. Click the button that says track package.

4. Select see all updates to check all delivery updates.
